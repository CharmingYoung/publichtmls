Auto-Renewal Service Agreement

1. Auto-Renewal

1.1 Members agree to automatically renew the service before the end of the service period and agree to pay the corresponding renewal fee.

1.2 The renewal fee will be based on the price agreed upon by the member at the initial purchase.

2. Canceling Auto-Renewal

2.1 Members have the right to cancel auto-renewal before the end of the service period.

2.2 Canceling auto-renewal must be notified to us in advance before the end of the service period.

3. Refund Policy

3.1 Once confirmed, renewal fees are non-refundable.

3.2 After canceling auto-renewal, the member will not be charged for renewal after the current service period ends, and the paid fees will not be refunded.

4. Notifications

4.1 We will send renewal notifications to the member through the contact information provided by the member.

4.2 Members are responsible for ensuring that the provided contact information is accurate and valid.
